PhD student under the supervision of [Manon Defosseux](https://helios2.mi.parisdescartes.fr/~mdefosse/) and [Pierre Tarrago](https://tarrago.perso.math.cnrs.fr).

My interests are probability theory (Rogers--Pitman criterion, Matsumoto--Yor processes, AX+B Markov chains) and geometry (Jordan algebras, symmetric cones, representation theory).

I also play the Celtic harp and am an enthusiast of Irish music.
